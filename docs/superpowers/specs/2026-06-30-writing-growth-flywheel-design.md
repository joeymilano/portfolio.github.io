# Joey Writing Growth Flywheel

## Purpose

Turn joeyzhao.cc from a static portfolio into Joey's owned content hub: a place where original writing compounds professional credibility, gives search and AI systems more useful public material, and routes readers between the website, LinkedIn, X, Xiaohongshu, and WeChat Channels.

The primary outcome is stronger senior product design and AI product job opportunities. Client collaboration remains a secondary conversion path.

## Experience

- Add `Writing / 写作` to the portfolio navigation and a compact selected-writing section on the home page.
- Build `/writing/` as an independent editorial publication using the approved "A" direction: serif-led headlines, restrained dark surfaces, generous whitespace, and one featured essay rather than a generic card grid.
- Keep articles focused and readable. Every article includes author identity, publication date, category, reading time, a concise takeaway, references where factual claims need support, related writing, and a clear route back to selected work.
- Chinese is the launch language. High-value professional essays can receive English editions later without blocking launch.

## Launch Collection

1. **AI 时代，设计师不能只卖页面** — positioning essay and featured story.
2. **麦当劳的座椅，究竟在优化什么？** — proven traffic topic rewritten as bounded analysis without unsupported claims.
3. **从一个 Brief 到 11 个平台：我如何设计「一鱼多吃」** — build-in-public product story grounded in the existing case study.
4. **电梯里的镜子，同时解决了四类问题** — inclusive and systems-design observation.
5. **作品集不是仓库，而是思维方式的证据链** — communication and design-method essay.

## Distribution Flywheel

- Treat the website article as the complete source. Platform versions are native adaptations, not duplicate full-text copies.
- LinkedIn carries professional summaries and selected English versions.
- X carries short insights, threads, and live build-in-public notes.
- Xiaohongshu carries Chinese visual explainers and proven traffic hooks.
- WeChat Channels carries 60–90 second video adaptations under `Joey的产品美学` (`sphmQz03lemSzBD`).
- Article pages link to Joey's platform profiles at the end, after the reading experience. X and LinkedIn also receive native share links.
- Social-to-site links use `utm_source`, `utm_medium=social`, and a stable article campaign slug.
- The site exposes LinkedIn, X, and Xiaohongshu in Person structured data. WeChat Channels remains visible as a name and copyable ID until a public profile URL or verified QR image is available.

## Discovery and Measurement

- Give every page a canonical URL, descriptive title/description, Open Graph/Twitter metadata, and `BlogPosting` JSON-LD.
- Add the writing hub and articles to `sitemap.xml`, publish an RSS feed, and keep `robots.txt` open to standard and AI search crawlers.
- Use semantic HTML and visible authorship. Avoid unsupported statistics and absolute causal claims.
- Add a small validation script that checks required files, canonical URLs, metadata, headings, article schema, and sitemap/feed coverage.

## Acceptance Criteria

- All six new public pages render without console errors and work at mobile and desktop widths.
- Portfolio navigation and home-page writing preview reach `/writing/`.
- Every launch article has unique metadata, canonical URL, schema, platform links, and related-content navigation.
- X points to `https://x.com/JoeyKonad`; video account copy identifies `Joey的产品美学` and `sphmQz03lemSzBD`.
- Sitemap and RSS include all launch articles.
- Validation and link checks pass without modifying unrelated screenshots or existing case-study assets.

1Background & Motivation

"The average person spends $273/month on subscriptions they forget about"

Most subscription trackers feel like spreadsheets with a UI. They're boring, guilt-trippy, and nobody actually wants to open them. I wanted to build something people would genuinely enjoy using - an app with personality that makes financial awareness fun.

The concept: a vampire character that feeds on your bills. The worse your spending, the more dramatic the vampire's reactions. AI doesn't just analyze - it roasts you, finds alternatives, and gives snarky motivational quotes for your no-spend streaks.

3Design Challenges

  • Making financial tracking feel fun, not guilt-inducing - the vampire theme turns spending awareness into entertainment
  • Balancing AI personality with usefulness - roasts need to be funny but the alternatives and advice must be genuinely helpful
  • Designing a cohesive dark-mode UI that works across tabs (bills, calendar, stats) while maintaining the spooky brand aesthetic
  • Creating a monetization model that doesn't betray the product's core promise: "the only subscription tracker that doesn't charge a subscription"

4Key Features I Designed & Built

Subscription Tracker with Multi-Currency
Add subscriptions in 6 currencies (USD, CNY, EUR, GBP, JPY, HKD) with automatic conversion. Monthly/yearly cycles, categorization (Entertainment, Productivity, Lifestyle), and real-time monthly total calculation.
AI Roast Advisor (Gemini 2.0 Flash)
Sends your subscription list to Gemini AI with a "snarky financial advisor" system prompt. Returns 3 sharp roasts and 1 practical saving tip. Bilingual support with different personality prompts per language.
AI Alternative Finder
AI analyzes each subscription and suggests 2-3 free or cheaper alternatives. Includes affiliate links to recommended services, creating a potential revenue stream.
No-Spend Streak Calendar
Gamified daily check-in calendar. Users tap today's date to mark a no-spend day. AI gives snarky/tsundere motivation based on streak length. Builds saving habits through positive reinforcement.
Shareable Spending Cards & PDF Export
Generate visual "vampire report" cards for social sharing. Premium print-optimized PDF reports with full subscription breakdown, charts, and calendar view.

What I Learned
This project pushed me to wear every hat: brand designer, UI/UX designer, frontend engineer, and product strategist. The biggest insight was that personality-driven design can make even boring tools engaging. People don't avoid tracking subscriptions because it's hard - they avoid it because it's joyless. By making AI genuinely funny and the UI delightful, the "chore" becomes something users look forward to. Building with Claude Code + Cursor also showed me how AI-assisted development can 10x the speed of solo projects without sacrificing design quality.

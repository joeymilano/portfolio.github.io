3My Role

Design
Brand identity, custom vampire mascot, dark-mode UI with glassmorphism, mobile-first responsive layout
Development
React 19 + Tailwind CSS 4, Vite build, Gemini AI integration, Chart.js visualizations, PWA with service worker
Product
Feature scoping, monetization strategy (freemium + one-time Pro), i18n for EN/ZH markets
Deployment
Cloudflare Pages hosting, CI/CD pipeline, PWA manifest and icons

4Design Challenges

  • Making financial tracking feel fun, not guilt-inducing - the vampire theme turns spending awareness into entertainment
  • Balancing AI personality with usefulness - roasts need to be funny but the alternatives and advice must be genuinely helpful
  • Designing a cohesive dark-mode UI that works across tabs (bills, calendar, stats) while maintaining the spooky brand aesthetic

5Key Features I Designed & Built

Subscription Tracker with Multi-Currency
6 currencies, one monthly view.
AI Roast Advisor (Gemini 2.5 Flash)
Snarky roasts + practical save tips.
AI Alternative Finder
Free and cheaper swaps in one tap.
No-Spend Streak Calendar
Daily check-ins that build momentum.
Shareable Spending Cards & PDF Export
One-click share cards and print-ready reports.

Deploy & Iterate

The project started as a weekend concept but evolved into a full product with monetization. I designed the custom vampire mascot as an SVG, built the entire frontend in React with Tailwind CSS, integrated Gemini AI for three distinct features, and deployed on Cloudflare Pages as a PWA.
